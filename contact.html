What is Flygo?
flygo (Le Travenues Technology Ltd.) was started by Rajnish Kumar and Aloke Bajpai in June 2007. ixigo is an intelligent, AI-based travel app, with a user base of over 170 million travellers. flygo uses Artificial Intelligence for deal discovery, personalized recommendations, dynamic marketing, airfare predictions, train delay information, PNR confirmation status, infrastructure cost optimization, business intelligence and for providing fully-automated customer service over chat and voice.

Why is ixigo different from other travel booking platforms?

How can I track refunds on ixigo?
Please follow the steps mentioned below to track your refund status:
1. Log in to your flygo account
2. Click on the customer service tab
3. Select your trip from the 'MyTrips' section
4. Click on the booking ID and check the refund status
How do I cancel my ticket on ixigo?
Please follow these simple steps to cancel your ticket on ixigo:
- Log in to ixigo
- Visit the 'My Trips' section on the app
- Select the desired booking, scroll down and click on the 'cancel ticket' tab to initiate cancellation.
- In case of partial cancellation, please select the desired passenger(s) and click on the cancel tab.
How do I get a refund on a ticket booking through flygo?
For refund of a confirmed booking, you need to place a cancellation request from the My Trips section of the ixigo app.
For refund related to a failed booking, the amount deducted from your account will be reversed in the source account within 7 days.
